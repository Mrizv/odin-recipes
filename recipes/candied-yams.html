Candied Yams

Description

This candied yams recipe is a simple and quick way to enjoy a Thanksgiving favorite! Canned yams or sweet potatoes may be used.

Ingredients

  1. Yams or Sweet Potatoes
  2. Butter
  3. Brown Sugar
  4. Ground Cinnamon
  5. Ground Nutmeg
  6. Mini Marshmallows

    7. Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Place yams in a large baking dish and dot with butter.
    3. Sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
    4. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
    5. Remove foil and stir. Continue baking for 25 minutes.
    6. Top with mini marshmallows and bake uncovered for 5 minutes.
